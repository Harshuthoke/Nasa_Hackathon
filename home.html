Unlocking Earth's Mysteries with EMIT

The EMIT sensor, launched in July 2022, is an incredible tool in space science. But how does it work? EMIT uses hyperspectral visible and short-wave infrared sensors to capture detailed images of Earth's surface from space. It's like taking high-resolution pictures of the planet.

EMIT has a fascinating history. It was developed by NASA to study mineral dust sources on Earth. These dust sources can have a big impact on our climate. By understanding them better, we can improve our climate models and take steps to protect our environment.
